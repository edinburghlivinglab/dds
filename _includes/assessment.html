Assessment

Coursework deadlines to be confirmed

Assessment for the course will be 100% coursework. There are no exams.

You will be assessed on two pieces of work. The main one will be a personal report which will describe the project that you have carried out, what your personal contribution was to the project, your goals and your conclusions. The second piece of work will be a group presentation of your team's project.
